The Vision for a Bountiful Harvest

Our five-year plan for agriculture is founded on the principles of collectivization and mechanization, aiming to create an abundance of food for the Soviet people and a surplus for the state. We envision a future where our fields are the most productive in the world.

The Role of the Kolkhoz

The collective farm, or Kolkhoz, is the cornerstone of our agricultural success. By pooling resources, labor, and land, our farmers work together for the common good, driving efficiency and ensuring that the fruits of their labor benefit the entire nation.
